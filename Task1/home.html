With experience across a wide range of design disciplines, she specializes in type-driven brand identities, packaging design, illustration, and typefaces. Her approach is strategic and research based, working intuitively with brands to distill their core messaging down to its clearest, most visceral form. Blending both art and design, she draws inspiration from a pastiche of art histories ranging from the classical to the modern to create something brand new. The outcome is lasting, pushing through the visual noise. Whether she is partnering with agencies or working brand-side, her aim is to build brands whose voice is memorable with a distinct point of view.
